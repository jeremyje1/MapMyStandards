Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the free trial?

The 7-day free trial includes full access to all A³E features with no limitations. You can upload evidence, run analytics, generate reports, and access all premium tools.

Do I need a credit card for the trial?

No credit card is required to start your free trial. You can explore the platform risk-free and decide if it's right for your institution.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. For monthly plans, cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. A³E is FERPA compliant and uses enterprise-grade security measures to protect your institutional data. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

Do you offer training and support?

Yes! All plans include comprehensive onboarding, training materials, and ongoing support. Annual plans include dedicated account management and priority support.
