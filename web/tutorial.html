  3. Filter Options: Show only required standards or those with gaps

Reviewing Mappings & Risk Assessment

After uploading documents, MapMyStandards automatically analyzes them and maps content to relevant standards. Here's how to review and refine these mappings:

The Mapping Process:

  1. Automated Analysis: AI reviews your documents
  2. Standard Matching: Content is mapped to relevant standards
  3. Confidence Scoring: Each mapping gets a confidence score
  4. Risk Identification: Gaps and weak areas are flagged

Reviewing Your Mappings:

For each mapping, you can:

  • ‚úì Accept the automated mapping
  • ‚úèÔ∏è Adjust the confidence level
  • ‚ûï Add additional standards
  • ‚ùå Remove incorrect mappings
  • üí¨ Add notes for your team

Understanding Risk Levels:

üî¥ High Risk

No evidence found

üü° Medium Risk

Weak or partial evidence

üü¢ Low Risk

Strong evidence present

üí° Tip: Focus on high-risk standards first. These need immediate attention before your accreditation review.

Generating Narratives

One of MapMyStandards' most powerful features is automatic narrative generation. These narratives synthesize your evidence into coherent responses for each standard.

How Narrative Generation Works:

  1. Select a standard that needs a narrative
  2. Review the mapped evidence documents
  3. Click "Generate Narrative"
  4. AI creates a comprehensive response
  5. Edit and refine as needed

What Makes a Good Narrative:

  • ‚úÖ Directly addresses the standard requirements
  • ‚úÖ Cites specific evidence and examples
  • ‚úÖ Uses your institution's terminology
  • ‚úÖ Includes measurable outcomes
  • ‚úÖ Demonstrates continuous improvement

Sample Generated Narrative:

"The institution demonstrates compliance with Standard 4.1 through its comprehensive governance structure. The Board of Trustees, composed of 15 members, operates under clearly defined bylaws (Evidence: BOT Bylaws 2024, pg. 3-7) that ensure independence from undue external influence..."

Editing Narratives:

After generation, you can:

  • Edit text directly in the platform
  • Add institution-specific details
  • Include additional evidence references
  • Adjust tone and style
  • Save multiple versions
