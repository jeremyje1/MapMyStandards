Privacy Policy

Protecting your institutional data and student privacy through comprehensive security measures and FERPA compliance.

Last Updated: January 1, 2025 | Effective Date: January 1, 2025

Introduction

MapMyStandards.ai ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of our users' data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you use our accreditation management platform.

As a service provider to higher education institutions, we understand the critical importance of protecting student educational records under the Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA) and maintaining the highest standards of data security.

MapMyStandards.ai is designed from the ground up to comply with FERPA regulations. We act as a "school official" under FERPA when processing educational records and maintain strict data protection standards that exceed federal requirements.

We collect and process the following types of institutional information:

Educational Records

When integrated with your Learning Management System (LMS) or student information systems, we may process de-identified, aggregated educational data including:

  • Course enrollment and completion statistics
  • Assessment and learning outcome achievement data
  • Academic program effectiveness metrics
  • Student success and retention indicators

Important: All student educational records are automatically de-identified during processing. No personally identifiable student information is stored or processed by our platform.

üîí Data Processing Principles

We process your data based on legitimate educational interests and contractual obligations. All data processing is limited to the minimum necessary to provide our services effectively.

Data Security & Protection

Technical Safeguards

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Access Controls: Multi-factor authentication and role-based access permissions
  • Infrastructure: AWS-hosted environment with enterprise-grade security
  • Monitoring: 24/7 security monitoring and threat detection
  • Backups: Automated, encrypted backups with secure recovery procedures

Administrative Safeguards

  • Employee background checks and security training
  • Strict data access policies and audit procedures
  • Regular security assessments and penetration testing
  • Incident response and breach notification procedures
  • Data retention and secure disposal policies

Physical Safeguards

  • Data centers with biometric access controls
  • 24/7 physical security monitoring
  • Environmental controls and redundant power systems
  • Secure destruction of physical storage media

Data Sharing & Disclosure

We Do Not Sell Your Data

MapMyStandards.ai does not sell, rent, or trade your institutional data or personal information to third parties for marketing or commercial purposes.

Limited Sharing Circumstances

We may share information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Trusted third-party vendors who assist in platform operations (cloud hosting, payment processing) under strict confidentiality agreements
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government regulation
  • Institutional Authorization: When you explicitly direct us to share information with specific third parties
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (with advance notice and continued privacy protections)

Student Data Protection

Educational records containing student information are subject to additional protections:

  • Never shared without explicit institutional authorization
  • Processed only for authorized educational purposes
  • Automatically de-identified before any analysis
  • Subject to enhanced security and access controls

Data Retention

Standard Retention Periods

  • Active Account Data: Retained for the duration of your subscription
  • Uploaded Documents: Retained until you delete them or close your account
  • Usage Analytics: Aggregated data retained for up to 2 years
  • Support Records: Retained for 3 years for service improvement

Account Closure

When you close your account:

  • All institutional data is deleted within 30 days
  • Educational records are immediately purged
  • Aggregated, de-identified analytics may be retained
  • Billing records are retained as required by law

Legal Hold

Data subject to legal proceedings or regulatory requirements may be retained longer as required by law, with appropriate security protections maintained.

International Data Transfers

Your data is primarily processed and stored in the United States using AWS infrastructure. If you are located outside the United States, please note:

  • Data transfers comply with applicable international privacy laws
  • We implement appropriate safeguards for cross-border transfers
  • Your data receives the same high level of protection regardless of location
  • We can accommodate data residency requirements for Enterprise customers

Children's Privacy

MapMyStandards.ai is designed for use by higher education institutions and their adult administrators. Our platform is not intended for direct use by students under 18 years of age.

While we may process de-identified educational records that could include information about students under 18, we:

  • Do not collect personal information directly from minors
  • Ensure all student data is de-identified before processing
  • Comply with FERPA requirements for educational records
  • Implement additional safeguards for any data involving minors

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. When we make changes:

  • We will post the updated policy on our website
  • We will notify you via email for material changes
  • We will provide 30 days' notice before changes take effect
  • Continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of updates

You can always find the most current version of our Privacy Policy at mapmystandards.ai/privacy.
