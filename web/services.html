A³E Engine Overview

Our proprietary Automated Accreditation Analytics Engine (A³E) combines artificial intelligence with deep accreditation expertise to streamline your compliance process.

Built on AWS Bedrock with Claude AI integration, A³E uses a comprehensive accreditation ontology developed through years of peer review experience.

  • Intelligent document parsing and evidence extraction
  • Multi-accreditor standard mapping and cross-referencing
  • Automated gap analysis with actionable recommendations
  • Citation verification and audit trail generation
  • FERPA-compliant data processing and storage

Multi-Accreditor Support

Our platform supports standards from all major accrediting bodies with specialized ontologies for each.

Regional Accreditors

SACSCOC, MSCHE, NECHE, HLC, NWCCU, WSCUC - Full standard mapping with citation verification

Specialized Accreditors

AACSB, ABET, CAEP, APA, LCME - Program-specific standards with professional requirements

State Agencies

SCHEV, HECC, CPE - State authorization and compliance requirements integrated

Federal Requirements

Title IV, FERPA, ADA - Federal compliance tracking with automated monitoring

Custom Standards

Institutional accreditors, international bodies - Custom ontology development available

Cross-Mapping

Automated identification of overlapping requirements across multiple accrediting bodies
